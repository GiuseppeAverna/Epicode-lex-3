Focaccia with caramelized onions, Cranberries and Brie

Wow your taste with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with several caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will becoem our favourite holiday starter!

Fresh Cranberrie Recipes That Go Beyiond Sauce

Ingredients

For the Focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil,for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh of water cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowls, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix togheter all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need actually to knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate yhe last of the flour ...
